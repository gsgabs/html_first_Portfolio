The best Movies according to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time

Mulan

Mulan movies deliver powerful messages about gender equality, self-identity, courage, family, leadership, tradition, and the value of honor and sacrifice.

Kung-Fu Panda

Kung Fu Panda movies impart important life lessons about self-discovery, perseverance, friendship, and the belief that anyone can achieve greatness when they embrace their uniqueness and follow their passion.

The Silet Voice

A heartwarming and emotionally charged anime film that delicately weaves a tale of redemption and understanding.
